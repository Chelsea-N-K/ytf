Internet Of Things(IOT)

Internet of Things course focuses on equipping learners with knowledge necessary to understand how physical objects that are embedded with sensors and softwares are able to exchange information with other devices within a network or through the internet. The track involves three stages ranging from beginners, through intermediate, all the way to advanced. They vary in complexity and level of detail but are all aimed at helping the learners get the most of it, where they get to learn the basics and reinforce it with a hand-on project.
