Scratch

Scratch is a visual programming language that helps kids aged 8-16 years to understand the basics of coding. When people learn to code in Scratch, they learn important strategies for solving problems, designing projects, and communicating ideas. With Scratch, you can program your own interactive stories, games and animations, and share your creations with others in an online community. Scratch helps young people to learn to think creatively, reason systematically, and work collaboratively- essential skills for life in the 21st century. Starting from Scratch
