Youth for Technology Foundation (YTF) is an international non-profit organization whose programs utilize technology to inspire youth and women in low-income communities and developing nations to create innovative solutions to the challenges they encounter in their own communities. YTF is empowering the next generation of leaders to enter the workforce with the skills they need to access employment or create their own entreprenurial opportunities in the fourth industrial revolution. For 19 years, award-winning YTF has provided technology training to over 1.6 million youth and women in education and entrepreneurship. YTF's programs have impacted 1.6 million youth and women across 5 countries. 55% of YTF Academy graduates pursue STEM related higher education and/or careers.
